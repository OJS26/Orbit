# Orbit

A clean, cosmic habit and routine tracker for iOS — built to help you stay consistent with your daily tasks.

Orbit lets you add recurring habits, tap to complete them each day, and watch your progress build over time. Designed with ADHD-friendly simplicity in mind — no clutter, just your tasks and your orbit.

---

## Features

- **Add recurring tasks** — daily, weekly, bi-weekly or monthly
- **Tap to complete** — satisfying one-tap completion with progress tracking
- **Multiple completions** — set tasks to require 2, 3 or more completions per period
- **Auto-reset** — tasks reset automatically at your chosen time
- **Streak tracking** — build and maintain streaks for each habit
- **Progress ring** — see your daily completion percentage at a glance
- **Smart sorting** — completed tasks sink to the bottom automatically
- **Home screen widget** — see your tasks without opening the app (small, medium and large)
- **Notifications** — get reminded when it's time to complete your tasks
- **Edit and delete tasks** — full task management
- **Onboarding** — clean first-launch experience

---

## Tech Stack

- **Swift** — primary language
- **SwiftUI** — UI framework
- **SwiftData** — local persistence
- **WidgetKit** — home screen widget
- **UserNotifications** — local push notifications
